# Episode Guide — *The Big Slide Day*

**Premise:** the weather follows kindness. Milo arrives with a personal rain cloud;
the world desaturates while he's unkind and blooms back to full sunshine when
Pip & Bean's kindness wins him over.

**Moral (spoken at 197s):** *"Kindness is sunshine you can share."*

## Cast

| character | design | voice | personality |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Narrator** | caption card, bottom center | PBS-style announcer | warm, wonder-filled |
| **Pip** | orange round bounce-ball, antenna | bright kid | bouncy, brave, has ideas |
| **Bean** | green sprout-headed bean | soft kid | builder, gentle, forgiving |
| **Milo** | purple coin-purse kid, rain cloud | gruff → warm kid | grumpy outside, lonely inside |

Speech bubbles carry the speaker's name in their color (`b-pip` orange, `b-bean`
green, `b-milo` purple); the narrator always speaks through the bottom caption card.
On-screen text never uses em/en dashes (house style).

## Act-by-act

### a1 — Sunshine opening (0:00–0:45)
Title sting over sunburst → meet Pip (bounce, bounce, bounce — boings on track 12/13)
and Bean (builds a sandcastle, "Ta-da!") → the Great Big Slide hero reveal ("Race you
to the top!"). BGM: Happy Whistling Ukulele.

### a2 — Milo arrives (0:45–1:36)
The gate: Milo walks in under his personal rain cloud. The slide line: "Out of my
way! ME first!" — he charges past (Pip & Bean wobble, elastic recover) and climbs
the ladder in 7 steps. The sandbox: grabs Bean's bucket, then **CRUNCH** (86.9s
thwack + screen shake + sand-particle burst + tier squash) — Bean's castle is
flattened, Bean swaps to the sad variant, two tears fall. The gray cloud slides over
the sun; a desaturating dim settles. BGM crossfades to Dreamy Piano Fantasy.

### a3 — Grumpy outside, lonely inside (1:36–2:41)
Pip comforts Bean by the flattened castle (the squashed-castle continuity prop).
Milo sits alone under the big oak. The insight scene: giant sad Milo, a heart
glowing through, headline *"grumpy outside... lonely inside"* — the episode's
emotional thesis. Pip's idea; the invitation: "Want to help?" Milo's drizzle stops —
first hope. Bean offers the favorite bucket.

### a4 — The frown flips (2:41–3:25)
Milo's frown wibbles, wobbles, turns upside down (cloud pops, sad→happy character
swap, purple brightens `#A8A0B5 → #9B7BE0`). Together they build the 4-tier mega
castle (tier pop-ins on boings); Bean plants the flag on top (bounce drop, then it
flutters in the wind). The slide train: all three ride the chute's **curve** down
(MotionPath), whoosh + "Wheeee!". The sun returns — chime, warm glow, hearts. BGM:
Funshine.

### a5 — Three magic moves + outro (3:25–4:14)
Recap cards pop in on a cream sunburst: **1 Take turns · 2 Share · 3 Say sorry**
(pops on each). Kindness line: "Being kind turns a grumpy day... into a SUNSHINE
day!" (win jingle). Outro: logo, "Thanks for playing", subscribe pill (it dims
politely while Milo — standing beneath it — says his goodbye), three-bubble
goodbye: Pip "Bye-bye!", Bean "See you soon, friends!", Milo "Bring your kindness!"
Fade out.

## Audio design

- **Voices** — fish.audio `s2.1-pro-free` (voice ids in `script.json`), generated by
  `gen_tts.py` (requires `FISH_AUDIO_API_KEY` env var), durations measured into
  `voice_manifest.json`.
- **BGM** (track 11, ≤0.16 vol) — ukulele until the CRUNCH lands, sad piano through
  act 3, Funshine from the frown-flip to the end. All CC0 (FreePD).
- **SFX** (tracks 12/13, CC0/OpenGameArt) — chime (title, sun return), boing
  (bounces, castle tiers), pop (cloud, rule cards), whoosh (slide train), thwack
  (CRUNCH), win jingle (kindness line). Track 13 exists only to lane overlapping
  boings.
