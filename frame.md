# Tumble Tunes — Pip & Bean: "The Big Slide Day"

**Concept angle:** a paper-cut storybook playground where the weather follows kindness —
Milo arrives with a personal gray cloud; the world dims while he's unkind and blooms
back to full sunshine when Pip & Bean's kindness wins him over.

## Palette
- Canvas cream `#FFF6E4` · sky `#C9E9F4` · ink `#4A3B2F`
- Accent coral `#FF7A45` · sun `#FFD34E`
- Grass `#A8D879` / hill `#8FC763` · sand `#F2D8A0` · slide red `#F26D6D`
- Pip orange `#FF9F4A` (line `#D97A24`) · Bean green `#7BC96F` (line `#4E9A44`)
- Milo sad `#A8A0B5` → Milo happy `#9B7BE0` (line `#6D51A8`)
- Caption card `#FFFDF6`, 3px ink border, storybook rounded 22px

## Type
- Display: **Baloo 2** (800) — chunky storybook headlines, logo, name tags
- Voice: **Patrick Hand** — speech bubbles + narrator caption band (≥38px)

## Rules
- Light canvas, warm; sadness shown by desaturated overlay tint, never dark bg
- Speech bubbles with tails for character lines; bottom caption card for narrator
- Every scene: sun/cloud layer + hills + ≥2 ambient decoratives (confetti dots,
  butterflies, grass tufts) + Tumble Tunes badge top-left
- Transitions: playful — circle iris (primary), vertical push, blur crossfade for the
  sad wind-down; pop-with-overshoot entrances (`back.out`)
